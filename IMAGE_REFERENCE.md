## ğŸ¨ **Image Optimization Tips**

1. **Slider Images**: 1920x1080px (Full HD)
2. **Service Cards**: 600x400px
3. **Team Photos**: 400x500px (Portrait)
4. **Blog Thumbnails**: 800x600px
5. **Testimonial Avatars**: 150x150px (Square)
6. **Logo**: PNG with transparent background

**Recommended Format**: 
- Photos: JPG (optimized, 80-85% quality)
- Logos/Icons: PNG (with transparency)
- Decorative elements: SVG (when possible)
